# Case p16 — fully supersonic isentropic flow (pₑ/p₀ = 0.16)

At this back-pressure ratio the flow accelerates through the throat, remains supersonic throughout the diverging section, and exits as a fully supersonic isentropic flow. No shock is present in the nozzle.

Initial pressure: p₀ = 10 kPa upstream of the throat; 1.6 kPa downstream, to accelerate convergence toward the choked solution.
